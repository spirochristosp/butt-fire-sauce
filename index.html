HOMEGROWN HEAT

Small-batch. Garden-grown. Absolutely unapologetic.

Butt Fire Sauce is a family-owned hot sauce made the old-fashioned way with peppers we grow ourselves. From jalapeños to ghost peppers to Carolina Reapers, every bottle starts in our organic garden and is hand-made in our own kitchen.

This isn’t a novelty sauce. It’s bold, deeply flavorful heat built from a carefully balanced blend of seriously spicy peppers. Hot enough to make you sweat. Good enough to keep you coming back.

No extracts. No shortcuts. Just real ingredients, real fire, and a sauce that respects flavor as much as heat.

Proceed with caution. And maybe a napkin.
